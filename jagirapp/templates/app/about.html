About us

Technological advancement has increased the use and demand of web application that helps in completion task easily from where you are. Among those many web application Online job portal is one of them. Not long ago, the task of job procurement was done by advertisement whether in newspaper or through any other media. Doing so was tedious for both employer and employee. Employee needed to read the advertisement, in some case the advisement might not contain full details, and them they need to visit the company. Likewise, company needed to spend money and time for this. To remove such difficulties online job portal was introduced.
